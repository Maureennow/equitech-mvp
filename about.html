About EquiTech Learning Hub

Empowering educators to build inclusive, tech-driven classrooms where every student can thrive.

Meet the Founder

Nwamaka Maureen Ndubuisi created EquiTech Learning Hub with a vision to merge equity and technology in education. Her work focuses on empowering teachers to use digital tools not just as teaching aids, but as bridges to meaningful, accessible learning for every student.

Building on her education in learning technologies and experience designing instructional resources, Nwamaka brings a creative and inclusive approach to educational design. She aims to help teachers and learners thrive through purpose-driven innovation.

My Mission

To close the digital equity gap in education by giving teachers access to tools, strategies, and resources that make technology a pathway to opportunity, not a barrier.
